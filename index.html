About Me

Hi, my name is Greer Viau. I am a Software Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science concentrating in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. I have over 4 years of professional, and over 8 years of personal experience building software projects.

In my personal projects, I like to experiment with some of the most cutting edge machine learning and deep learning technologies. You can check them out below.
